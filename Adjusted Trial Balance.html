ADJUSTED TRIAL BALANCE PROCESS


1. All adjustments: debits and credits posted to the t/b account using adjustment columns

2. Add or subtract the adjustment amount from the amount in the trial balance

3. The net adjustment amount for each line in placed in the trial balance column

4. add accounts if not present in the original trial balance to the bottom of the account list

5. sum individually the amounts in the both the debit & credit column and place total on bottom

6. the total debits must equal the total credits
